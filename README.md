1. Introduction
Data Science is one of the fastest-growing and most impactful fields in the modern digital era. With the explosion of data from industries such as healthcare, finance, e-commerce, and artificial intelligence, the demand for skilled data scientists continues to rise.
This project, “Data Science Future Scope Analysis”, focuses on analyzing future trends related to:
•	Salary growth
•	Job opportunities
•	Industry demand
•	Market growth rate
The analysis is presented using Power BI dashboards, providing a clear and visual understanding of how Data Science will evolve in the coming years.

4. Dataset Description
The dataset used in this project contains future-oriented data related to Data Science careers.
Key columns include:
•	Year – Timeline from 2025 to 2030
•	Average_Salary_USD – Average annual salary in USD
•	Growth_Rate_Percent – Industry growth rate
•	Jobs_Millions – Number of jobs in millions
•	Industry – High-demand sectors
This dataset helps in forecasting future opportunities in the Data Science field.

6. Key Insights and Analysis
Salary Analysis:
The average salary of Data Scientists shows a steady increase from 2025 to 2030. By 2030, salaries are expected to reach up to $118,000 annually, indicating strong financial growth in this career.

Job Market Growth:
The number of Data Science jobs is expected to grow from approximately 12 million to 22 million globally by 2030, highlighting massive employment opportunities.

Growth Rate:
The industry growth rate increases consistently from 18% to 24%, showing that Data Science is expanding faster than many traditional career fields.

Industry Demand:
Industries such as IT, Finance, Healthcare, E-commerce, and Artificial Intelligence are the top recruiters of Data Science professionals.
